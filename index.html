Joining and teaming-up

Joining and teaming-up , different children churches , with others locally and internationally , is a very vibrant encouragement to many children church teachers ; it keeps them going.

This is the Union of Sunday School Churches also known as the Sunday School Association,plays a significant role in promoting Christian education and unity among churches.Here are some importance of the Union of Sunday School Churches:

  • Unity among churches:Fosters collaboration and unity among churches promoting a sense of belonging and shared purpose.
  • Standardized curriculum:Develops and provides standardized Sunday School curriculum, ensuring consistency in Christian education.
  • Resource sharing:Facilitates sharing of resources, expertise,and best practices among member churches.
